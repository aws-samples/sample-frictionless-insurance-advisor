## Riders

- **Future Increase Option:** allows the insured to raise the monthly benefit
  by up to $1,000 every 3 years without new medical underwriting

  is unable to perform 2 or more activities of daily living
- **Return of Premium Rider:** refunds 50% of premiums paid if no claim is
  made within any 10-year window

## Policy Notes

This long-term disability policy provides a guaranteed monthly benefit
during periods of qualifying disability, subject to the 90-day elimination
period and the 5-year benefit period. Benefits are payable in arrears on
the last day of each month for which the insured is disabled. Coverage
terminates at age 65 or upon recovery, whichever is earlier.
