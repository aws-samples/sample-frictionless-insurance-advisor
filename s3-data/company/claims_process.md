# Unicorn Insurance Claims Process

This document describes how we handle claims: what the customer needs to do, what we do, how long it typically takes, what documents we need, and what to do if the customer disagrees with our decision. It is written for advisors and applies across our lines of business. Specific policy wordings always take precedence over this summary.

## Principles

We handle claims against four commitments:

- **Fair** — decisions follow the policy wording and the evidence, not arbitrary judgment.
- **Fast** — we set clear target timelines per claim type and publish our performance against them.
- **Clear** — every decision comes with a written explanation of what is covered, what is not, and why.
- **Proactive** — where the policy provides for emergency services, we arrange them without waiting for the full claim to be assessed.

## How Claims Work

Our end-to-end process has three stages: **submit, assess, settle.**

### Stage 1 — Submit

Customers can open a claim through any of the following channels:

- The **claims hotline** (24/7, numbers per market — see customer service documentation).
- The **mobile app** ("Start a claim" on the home screen).
- The **customer portal** on the web.
- **In-app secure messaging** or **email** to `claims@unicorninsurance.example`.
- **Through their advisor**, with the customer's consent.

At submission we capture the basic facts: policy number, date of loss, what happened, who was involved, and how to contact the customer. We issue a claim reference number immediately and confirm next steps in writing the same day.

For urgent situations (motor accident with injury, house fire, medical emergency abroad) the hotline also dispatches assistance — towing, temporary accommodation, medical evacuation — as provided in the policy, without waiting for the formal assessment.

### Stage 2 — Assess

A claims handler is assigned, typically within one business day. The handler:

- Confirms the policy is active and the event/condition is covered.
- Requests supporting documents (see line-specific requirements below).
- Contacts third parties if needed (hospital, garage, police report).
- Makes a coverage decision based on policy wording.

### Stage 3 — Settle

Once approved:
- **Cashless/direct billing (health)**: We pay the provider directly via eLOG or post-treatment settlement.
- **Reimbursement**: Paid to policyholder's bank account within the line-specific timeline.
- **Lump-sum (CI/Life)**: Single payment to nominated beneficiary/policyholder.
- **Monthly benefit (disability)**: First payment within 5 business days of approval, then monthly.

## Health Insurance Claims — Specific Details

### Cashless (Direct Billing) Claims

**How it works:**
1. Customer presents insurance card at any of our 800+ panel hospitals/clinics.
2. Hospital contacts us for Electronic Letter of Guarantee (eLOG).
3. We issue eLOG:
   - **Planned admissions**: Within 2 hours of request.
   - **Emergency admissions**: Within 30 minutes.
4. Hospital treats patient. We settle directly with the hospital.
5. If final bill exceeds eLOG amount, balance processed as reimbursement claim (5 business days).

**Pre-authorisation (for planned procedures):**
- Customer submits pre-authorisation request via app (upload specialist referral letter and estimated cost).
- We respond within 2 business days with approval, request for more information, or decline with reason.
- Pre-authorisation is recommended for all planned procedures above $5,000 to avoid post-treatment disputes.

**vs BigRival's Process:**
- BigRival requires phone or in-person pre-authorisation (no app).
- BigRival eLOG takes 4-6 hours for planned admissions.
- BigRival's panel list is not searchable online (must call agent).

**vs StarInsure:**
- StarInsure does not offer health insurance or cashless claims at all.

### Reimbursement Claims (Health)

**How it works:**
1. Customer pays the healthcare provider directly.
2. Customer submits claim via app (photo of receipts + treatment details) within 30 days of treatment.
3. We process and reimburse within **5 business days** for straightforward claims.
4. Complex claims (above $10,000 or requiring medical review): Up to 15 business days.
5. Payment to policyholder's registered bank account.

**Required documents:**
- Original itemised receipts (photos via app accepted).
- Medical report or referral letter (for specialist consultations).
- Discharge summary (for hospitalisation).
- Police report (for accident-related claims).

### Critical Illness Claims

**How it works:**
1. Customer (or representative) submits claim notification via app, phone, or advisor.
2. Required: specialist medical report confirming diagnosis meets policy severity criteria.
3. Assessment within **10 business days**.
4. Lump-sum payment within 3 business days of approval.
5. For multi-stage policies: early/intermediate stage payments do NOT terminate the policy.

**Survival period:** 14 days (customer must survive 14 days after diagnosis for claim to be valid).

### Life Insurance Claims

**How it works:**
1. Beneficiary contacts us via hotline, app, or advisor.
2. Required: death certificate, policy document, beneficiary ID.
3. Assessment within **10 business days** (straightforward cases).
4. Payment to nominated beneficiary's bank account.
5. 97.5% of life claims are paid (industry-leading).

## Disputes and Appeals

If a customer disagrees with a claims decision:
1. **Internal review**: Request within 30 days. Reviewed by senior claims manager (not original handler). Decision within 10 business days.
2. **Ombudsman referral**: If still unsatisfied, escalate to the relevant industry ombudsman.
3. **Legal action**: As a last resort, subject to policy arbitration clause.

We publish our appeal overturn rate: **18%** of appealed decisions are overturned or revised in the customer's favour, demonstrating our willingness to reassess.
