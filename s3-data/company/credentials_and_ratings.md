# Unicorn Insurance Credentials and Ratings

This document summarizes our regulatory status, financial-strength ratings, and industry certifications. Advisors can use it to answer questions about whether we are regulated, how financially sound we are, and what standards we operate to. All figures and ratings are current as of the last annual review.

## Regulatory Licenses

We operate as a group of licensed insurance entities, each authorized by the relevant supervisory authority in its home jurisdiction. We do not write insurance business in any country where we are not properly licensed.

### Asia-Pacific

- **Unicorn Insurance (Singapore) Pte. Ltd.** — the group's primary operating entity, licensed by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) as a direct insurer for both life and general business. Acts as group supervisor under the MAS consolidated supervision framework.
- **Unicorn Insurance (Hong Kong) Limited** — authorized by the Insurance Authority of Hong Kong.
- **Unicorn Insurance Australia Pty Ltd** — authorized by the Australian Prudential Regulation Authority (APRA) and holds an Australian Financial Services Licence from ASIC.
- **Unicorn Insurance (UAE) PJSC** — licensed by the UAE Central Bank (insurance supervision).

### European Economic Area

- **Unicorn Insurance Europe dac** — authorized and regulated by the Central Bank of Ireland. Passported under Solvency II into all EEA member states where we write business. Reference number available on the Central Bank's public register.
- **Unicorn Insurance UK Limited** — authorized by the Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA) and regulated by the PRA and the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) in the United Kingdom.

### Americas

- **Unicorn Insurance Canada** — federally incorporated and regulated by the Office of the Superintendent of Financial Institutions (OSFI), with provincial market-conduct oversight.
- **Unicorn Insurance USA, Inc.** — domiciled in Delaware and licensed as an admitted carrier in 14 U.S. states. Subject to state-level supervision by each state's Department of Insurance and to group supervision under the NAIC framework.

### Distribution licensing

Advisors representing Unicorn Insurance must hold the intermediary license required in their own jurisdiction (for example, IDD-aligned registration in the EEA, FCA authorization in the UK, MAS representative status in Singapore, or state producer licensing in the U.S.). We verify advisor credentials at onboarding and on an annual refresh.

## Financial-Strength Ratings

We are rated by three independent agencies. Ratings apply to the core operating entities of the group and are reviewed at least annually. Ratings may differ slightly by entity; the figures below reflect the main operating companies.

### AM Best

- **Financial Strength Rating:** A+ (Superior)
- **Long-Term Issuer Credit Rating:** aa-
- **Outlook:** Stable
- **Last affirmed:** within the last 12 months

AM Best cites our disciplined underwriting, diversified product mix, and strong risk-adjusted capitalization (as measured by BCAR) as key rating drivers.

### S&P Global Ratings

- **Insurer Financial Strength Rating:** AA- 
- **Outlook:** Stable
- **Last affirmed:** within the last 12 months

S&P points to our competitive position in core European markets, conservative investment portfolio, and capital adequacy well above the 'AA' confidence level.

### Moody's Investors Service

- **Insurance Financial Strength Rating:** Aa3
- **Outlook:** Stable
- **Last affirmed:** within the last 12 months

Moody's highlights our consistent underwriting profitability, prudent reinsurance program, and stable earnings across the cycle.

### What these ratings mean

All three agencies place us in their "very strong" or "superior" tiers for ability to meet policyholder obligations. Ratings are the agencies' opinions, not a guarantee, and they can change. Advisors can direct customers to each agency's website for the latest public rating report.

## Solvency and Capital

In addition to agency ratings, we report our regulatory capital position to supervisors and, where required, to the public.

- **Solvency II SCR coverage ratio (EEA entities):** approximately 195% at the most recent year-end, well above the 100% regulatory minimum.
- **UK Solvency II SCR coverage:** approximately 190%.
- **MAS CAR ratio (Singapore):** above regulatory minimum with healthy buffer.
- **APRA PCA coverage (Australia):** above regulatory minimum with healthy buffer.
- **Canadian LICAT / MCT ratios:** above OSFI supervisory targets.

We publish a Solvency and Financial Condition Report (SFCR) each year for our EEA and UK entities, and equivalent disclosures where local regimes require them.

## Industry Certifications and Memberships

### Information security

- **ISO/IEC 27001** — certified for the information security management system covering our core policy, claims, and customer platforms. Certification is maintained through annual surveillance audits by an accredited certification body.
- **SOC 2 Type II** — reports issued annually for our customer-facing digital platform and advisor portal, covering the Security, Availability, and Confidentiality trust service criteria.
- **PCI DSS** — compliant for the payment environment used to process card transactions, with annual attestation.

### Privacy

- **ISO/IEC 27701** — certified for the privacy information management system, extending the 27001 scope to personal data handling. Supports GDPR, UK GDPR, and equivalent regimes.
- Registered with data protection authorities in all jurisdictions where we hold personal data (for example, the Irish Data Protection Commission, the UK ICO, and the PDPC in Singapore).

### Business continuity and quality

- **ISO 22301** — certified for business continuity management covering our claims operations and digital platform.
- **ISO 9001** — certified for the quality management system covering our group customer operations.

### Industry associations

We are members of the main insurance associations in our regions, which shape market practice and codes of conduct:

- Insurance Ireland
- Association of British Insurers (ABI)
- Insurance Europe (via national associations)
- General Insurance Association of Singapore (GIA) and Life Insurance Association of Singapore (LIA)
- Insurance Council of Australia
- Insurance Bureau of Canada and Canadian Life and Health Insurance Association

## Complaints and Ombudsman

We are in scope for the independent dispute-resolution scheme in each market where we write business, including:

- **Ireland:** Financial Services and Pensions Ombudsman (FSPO)
- **United Kingdom:** Financial Ombudsman Service (FOS)
- **Singapore:** Financial Industry Disputes Resolution Centre (FIDReC)
- **Australia:** Australian Financial Complaints Authority (AFCA)
- **Canada:** General Insurance OmbudService (GIO) and OmbudService for Life and Health Insurance (OLHI)

Our internal complaints process and escalation path are described in the customer service documentation.

## How to Verify

Advisors and customers can independently verify our licensing and ratings:

- Regulator registers (Central Bank of Ireland, FCA/PRA, MAS, APRA, OSFI, state DOIs).
- Rating agency websites (AM Best, S&P Global Ratings, Moody's) for public rating pages.
- Certification body directories for ISO certifications.
- Our annual report and SFCR for financial disclosures.

If an advisor or customer has a specific verification request, our compliance team can provide the relevant reference numbers and certificates on request through the contact channels in the customer service documentation.
