# StarBasic Forever (StarInsure)
### Type: Simplified Whole Life Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

StarBasic Forever is a small-face-amount simplified-issue whole life product, primarily positioned as final-expense coverage. Face amounts range from approximately $5,000 to $50,000, with level premiums and guaranteed lifetime coverage. Cash value accumulates at a contractually guaranteed rate but is intentionally small relative to traditional whole life products.

**Key features**
- No medical exam, simplified underwriting via short health questionnaire
- Issue ages typically from 45 to 80, aimed at the final-expense segment
- Level premiums for life, no step-ups
- Small guaranteed cash value component
- App-based purchase and policy delivery
- Limited rider menu (typically accidental death only)

**Pricing tier:** Budget
