# StarBasic Shield (StarInsure)
### Type: Critical Illness Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

StarBasic Shield is StarInsure's simplified critical illness product, sold entirely through the app with no medical exam required. It delivers a lump-sum payment on diagnosis of a small set of covered conditions, positioned as a budget safety-net product for younger and price-sensitive customers. The product trades depth for accessibility: covered conditions are limited to the most common serious illnesses, and face amounts are modest relative to fully underwritten critical illness coverage.

### Premium Positioning
- **Budget tier**: 40-60% cheaper than Unicorn Health Critical, but covers only 3 conditions vs Unicorn's up to 20+.
- **No multi-policy discount** available (StarInsure doesn't offer health insurance or bundled products).
- **Annual premium increases**: Historically 8-12% per year due to narrow, high-risk pool and limited data.
- **Stepped premiums only** — premiums increase with age at each renewal (no level premium option).

## Coverage Details

### What's Covered
- **Cancer** (invasive, excluding early-stage skin cancers)
- **Heart attack** (acute myocardial infarction meeting severity criteria)
- **Stroke** (with permanent neurological deficit lasting 24+ hours)
- That's it — **only 3 conditions covered**.

### What's NOT Covered (unlike Unicorn Health Critical)
- Kidney failure / major organ transplant: NOT covered
- Multiple sclerosis: NOT covered
- Parkinson's disease: NOT covered
- Major burns: NOT covered
- Early-stage / intermediate-stage critical illness: NOT covered
- Recovery care: NOT covered
- Child critical illness: NOT covered

### Outpatient/Dental/Optical
- **StarBasic Shield does NOT provide any health insurance coverage.**
- No outpatient coverage, no hospitalisation cover, no dental, no optical.
- It is a pure lump-sum CI product — NOT a health plan.
- StarInsure does not sell a comprehensive health insurance product at all.

### Mental Health
- **Not covered.** No mental health benefits of any kind.

### Maternity
- **Not covered.** No pregnancy or maternity benefits.

### Alternative Treatments
- **Not covered.** No TCM, acupuncture, or alternative medicine.

## Waiting Periods

| Benefit | Waiting Period |
|---------|---------------|
| All covered conditions | **90 days** from policy inception |
| Pre-existing conditions | **Permanently excluded** (not covered even after a waiting period) |
| Survival period | 14 days (must survive 14 days after diagnosis) |

- **Pre-existing conditions are permanently excluded** — unlike Unicorn (12-month waiting then covered) and BigRival (12-month waiting then covered).
- No portability credit from other insurers.
- **Policy automatically terminates after one claim** — single payout only, no multi-stage benefit.
- Coverage terms: 10-year or 20-year only (no lifetime option).

## Claims Process

### No Cashless Option
- **Reimbursement/lump-sum only** — there is no cashless hospital facility because StarBasic Shield is not a health/hospitalisation product.
- Claim submission via app only (no phone, no email, no paper).
- Processing: **3-5 business days** for straightforward claims (positive: fast processing due to simple criteria).
- Payment: Direct bank transfer only.
- Required documentation: medical report from treating specialist confirming diagnosis.

### Claims Settlement Ratio
- CI claims settlement: **88.5%** (lower than industry average).
- High rejection rate primarily due to: condition not meeting severity criteria (6%), pre-existing condition exclusion (3.5%), diagnosis within waiting period (2%).

## Income Replacement
- **Lump-sum payout only** ($10,000-$50,000).
- **No income replacement, no disability cover, no hospital cash benefit.**
- Payout amount is modest and may not cover actual treatment costs for cancer (average treatment costs $50,000-$200,000+).
- Policy terminates after claim — no ongoing benefit.

**Key features**
- Face amounts from approximately $10,000 to $50,000 in fixed tiers
- No medical exam, simplified underwriting via short health questionnaire
- Coverage issued in minutes for qualifying applicants aged 25-55
- Covered conditions limited to cancer, heart attack, and stroke (no extended conditions list)
- Fixed 10-year or 20-year terms, no lifetime option
- Stepped premiums (increase with age)
- Single beneficiary configuration by default
- Digital policy documents, no paper
- No optional riders

**Pricing tier:** Budget
