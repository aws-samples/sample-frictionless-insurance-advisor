# StarBasic Travel (StarInsure)
### Type: Single-Trip Travel Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

StarBasic Travel is a minimalist single-trip travel insurance product, primarily focused on emergency medical coverage and trip cancellation for standard leisure travel. Coverage caps are typically lower than those of specialist travel insurers.

**Key features**
- Single-trip only, no annual multi-trip option
- App-based purchase up to the moment of departure
- Fixed medical coverage cap at approximately $100,000
- Limited baggage and delay coverage

**Pricing tier:** Budget
