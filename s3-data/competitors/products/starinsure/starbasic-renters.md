# StarBasic Renters (StarInsure)
### Type: Renters Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

StarBasic Renters covers personal property and personal liability for tenants. Coverage amounts for personal property typically reach up to approximately $50,000, with liability limits up to $100,000. The product is positioned as a compliance tool for tenants whose landlords require renters coverage.

**Key features**
- App-based purchase with coverage active the same day
- Standardised coverage tiers with minimal customisation
- Low deductibles on the standard configuration
- Digital proof-of-insurance delivered to landlords on request

**Pricing tier:** Budget
