# StarBasic Auto (StarInsure)
### Type: Auto Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

StarBasic Auto provides essential auto insurance with a focus on legally required third-party liability coverage. Optional comprehensive and collision tiers are available at additional cost, but defaults are set to the legal minimum. Liability limits typically reach up to approximately $100,000 bodily injury and $50,000 property damage on the standard configuration.

**Key features**
- Fully app-based quote and bind, typically completed in under five minutes
- Policy documents issued immediately on purchase
- Monthly premium payments with no annual commitment
- In-app claims submission with photo upload

**Pricing tier:** Budget
