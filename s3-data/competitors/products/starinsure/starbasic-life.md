# StarBasic Life (StarInsure)
### Type: Simplified Term Life Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

StarBasic Life is a simplified-issue term life product with coverage amounts up to approximately $250,000 and terms of 10, 15, or 20 years. Underwriting is limited to a short online questionnaire with no medical exam required.

**Key features**
- No medical exam underwriting path
- Coverage issued in minutes for qualifying applicants
- Single beneficiary configuration by default
- Digital policy documents, no paper

**Pricing tier:** Budget
