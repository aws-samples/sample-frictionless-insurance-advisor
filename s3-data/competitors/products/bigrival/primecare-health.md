# PrimeCare Health (BigRival)
### Type: Supplemental Health Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

PrimeCare Health is BigRival's supplemental health product, sitting alongside public and employer-provided health plans. It is positioned at the **premium pricing tier** with comprehensive coverage but higher costs. The product trades price efficiency for brand reputation, broad hospital access, and traditional in-person service.

### Premium Positioning
- **Premium tier pricing**: Typically 15-25% higher than Unicorn's HealingHorn Health and 40-60% higher than StarInsure's offerings.
- **Claims-based loading**: After a major claim ($10,000+), BigRival applies a premium loading of up to 30% at next renewal. This loading persists for 3 consecutive claim-free years before reverting to standard rate.
- **No multi-policy discount** for combining with other BigRival products.
- **Loyalty discount**: 5% off after 5 continuous years of membership (lower than Unicorn's 10% after 2 years).
- **Annual premium increases**: Historically 5-8% per year, driven by older demographic portfolio and higher claims frequency.

## Coverage Details

### Outpatient Treatments
- **Enhanced and Premier tiers include outpatient**:
  - GP consultations: Covered up to $100/visit, max 20 visits/year
  - Specialist consultations: Covered up to $250/visit, max 10 visits/year
  - Diagnostic imaging: Covered at 80% up to $4,000/year
  - Prescription medications: Covered up to $2,500/year
  - Physiotherapy: Covered up to $150/session, max 15 sessions/year
- **Standard tier**: Post-hospitalisation outpatient only (60 days after discharge)

### Dental Coverage
- **Premier tier includes dental**:
  - Preventive (cleaning, check-ups): 2 visits/year, up to $250/visit
  - Restorative (fillings, crowns): Covered at 60%, max $2,000/year
  - Emergency dental: Covered at 100% up to $5,000
  - Orthodontics: Not covered
- **Enhanced tier**: Emergency dental only
- **Standard tier**: No dental

### Optical/Vision
- **Premier tier**: Annual exam ($200) + $350 frames/lenses every 2 years
- **Enhanced and Standard**: No optical coverage

### Mental Health
- **Premier tier**:
  - Inpatient psychiatric: Up to $12,000/year
  - Outpatient: Up to $180/session, max 15 sessions/year
  - No tele-therapy coverage (in-person only)
- **Enhanced tier**:
  - Inpatient psychiatric: Up to $6,000/year
  - Outpatient: Up to $120/session, max 8 sessions/year
- **Standard tier**:
  - Inpatient psychiatric: Up to $3,000/year (emergencies only)
  - No outpatient mental health

### Maternity
- **Available as add-on (Premier tier only)** — additional $2,800-$4,500/year depending on age:
  - Normal delivery: Up to $10,000
  - Caesarean: Up to $18,000
  - Complications: Covered at 80% up to policy limit
  - Pre-natal: Routine visits covered, max 12 visits
  - **18-month waiting period** (longer than industry standard)
  - IVF pregnancies: Not covered for delivery or complications
  - Post-natal: 4 weeks follow-up

### Alternative Treatments
- **Not covered under any tier.** BigRival's underwriting guidelines explicitly exclude alternative and complementary medicine. TCM, acupuncture, chiropractic, and naturopathy are all excluded.

## Claims Process

### Cashless Claims
- Available at BigRival's national agent/hospital network — approximately 600 facilities.
- eLOG processing time: **4-6 hours for planned admissions**, 1 hour for emergencies.
- Pre-authorisation via phone or in-person at agent office (no app-based pre-auth).
- Panel hospital list not easily accessible online — customers must call or visit agent.

### Reimbursement
- Submit via paper form or email (no app submission).
- Standard processing: **10-15 business days** for straightforward claims.
- Complex claims: Up to 30 business days.
- Payment via cheque (direct bank transfer available on request).

### Claims Settlement Ratio
- Overall health claims settlement: **91.8%** (below industry average of 94%).
- Top rejection reasons: pre-existing condition non-disclosure (4.2%), policy exclusions (2.1%), documentation incomplete (1.9%).
- Appeals process available but takes 30-45 days.

## Income Replacement
- **No income replacement under PrimeCare Health.**
- **No hospital cash benefit add-on available** for PrimeCare.
- Customers must purchase CareerShield Disability separately for income protection.
- **Lump-sum critical illness**: Not available as a PrimeCare add-on; must purchase separately.

**Key features**
- In-network and out-of-network reimbursement options
- Direct billing available at partnered hospitals
- Dental and vision add-on tiers (Premier only)
- Long-standing relationships with large national hospital groups
- Broad underwriting tolerance for older applicants and higher-risk profiles

**Pricing tier:** Premium
