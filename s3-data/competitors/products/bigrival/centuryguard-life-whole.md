# CenturyGuard Life Whole (BigRival)
### Type: Whole Life Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

CenturyGuard Life Whole is the permanent variant of BigRival's flagship life insurance line. It combines lifetime coverage with a guaranteed cash value component that grows at a contractually fixed minimum rate. Premiums are level for life, and the policy builds cash value that the policyholder can borrow against or surrender.

**Key features**
- Coverage up to approximately $3 million
- Level lifetime premiums, with accelerated 10-pay, 20-pay, and paid-up-at-65 options available
- Guaranteed minimum cash value growth rate
- Dividend participation on eligible policies, with dividend-use options (cash, reduce premiums, accumulate, paid-up additions)
- Broad underwriting tolerance for older applicants (frequently up to age 75)
- In-person medical underwriting through a country-wide panel of examiners
- Multiple rider options including waiver of premium, accidental death, and guaranteed insurability
- Policy loans available against accumulated cash value at a guaranteed loan rate

**Pricing tier:** Premium
