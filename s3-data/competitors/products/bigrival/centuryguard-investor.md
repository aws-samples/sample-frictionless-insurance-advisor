# CenturyGuard Investor (BigRival)
### Type: Variable Life Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

CenturyGuard Investor is BigRival's variable life product, combining permanent coverage with a menu of professionally managed investment sub-accounts. Coverage is available up to approximately $5 million. Cash value fluctuates with the performance of the chosen sub-accounts and is not guaranteed. The product is sold by prospectus only, reflecting its investment-security nature.

**Key features**
- Curated sub-account menu spanning equity, fixed income, balanced, and international strategies
- Choice of death benefit options including level face amount or face plus cash value
- Dollar cost averaging program available as a standard feature
- Sold by prospectus through licensed BigRival agents who hold both insurance and securities licenses
- Optional guaranteed minimum death benefit rider to preserve a floor on the face amount

**Pricing tier:** Premium
