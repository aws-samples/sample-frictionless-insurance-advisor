# CareerShield Disability (BigRival)
### Type: Disability Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

CareerShield Disability is BigRival's income protection line, designed for professionals and primary household earners who need coverage during periods of disability. The product offers long benefit periods, configurable monthly benefits, and own-occupation definitions for professional and specialist roles. Underwriting is conducted through BigRival's national examiner panel with in-person medical exams as the default.

**Key features**
- Benefit periods of 2 years, 5 years, 10 years, or until retirement age
- Waiting (elimination) periods of 30, 60, 90, 180, or 365 days
- Monthly benefits from $2,000 to $20,000, capped at 60-70% of pre-disability income
- Occupation Class 1 through Class 5, with specialist class for medical, legal, and dental professionals
- Own Occupation definition available for the full benefit period on qualifying classes
- Short-Term, Long-Term, or combined policy structures
- Established claims adjudication with in-person case managers on long-tenure claims
- Optional riders: Cost of Living Adjustment (COLA), Future Purchase Option, Catastrophic Disability Benefit, Retirement Protection

**Pricing tier:** Premium
