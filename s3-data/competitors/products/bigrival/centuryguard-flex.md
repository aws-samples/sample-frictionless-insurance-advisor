# CenturyGuard Flex (BigRival)
### Type: Universal Life Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

CenturyGuard Flex is BigRival's universal life offering, providing permanent coverage with flexible premium payments and an adjustable death benefit. Coverage is available up to approximately $4 million. Cash value is credited at a declared interest rate that is reset periodically, with a contractually guaranteed minimum floor.

**Key features**
- Flexible premium payments within contractual limits, allowing customers to vary contributions year to year
- Choice between level (Option A) and increasing (Option B) death benefit
- Guaranteed minimum crediting rate on cash value
- Optional no-lapse guarantee rider to protect against policy lapse in low interest rate environments
- In-person underwriting through BigRival's national examiner panel

**Pricing tier:** Premium
