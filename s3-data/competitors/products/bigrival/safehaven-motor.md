# SafeHaven Motor (BigRival)
### Type: Auto Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

SafeHaven Motor is BigRival's auto insurance line, covering third-party liability, comprehensive, and collision. Coverage extends to personal, commercial, and fleet use. Liability limits reach up to approximately $500,000 bodily injury and $500,000 property damage.

**Key features**
- Coverage available for classic and collectible vehicles under a separate sub-line
- Optional accident forgiveness on renewal
- Rental reimbursement and roadside assistance available as riders
- Established claims adjuster network with in-person inspections

**Pricing tier:** Premium
