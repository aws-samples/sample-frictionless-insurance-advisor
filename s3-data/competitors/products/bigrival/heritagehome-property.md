# HeritageHome Property (BigRival)
### Type: Home and Contents Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

HeritageHome Property covers owner-occupied homes, rental properties, condominiums, and vacation homes. Dwelling coverage is available up to approximately $3 million. Personal property coverage can be extended to 100% of dwelling value.

**Key features**
- Replacement cost coverage on dwelling and contents
- Scheduled personal property riders for jewellery, fine art, and collectibles
- Optional flood, earthquake, and equipment breakdown endorsements
- Guaranteed rebuild cost option on qualifying older homes

**Pricing tier:** Premium
