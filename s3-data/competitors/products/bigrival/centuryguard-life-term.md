# CenturyGuard Life Term (BigRival)
### Type: Term Life Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

CenturyGuard Life Term is the term variant of BigRival's flagship life insurance line. Coverage is available up to approximately $3 million, with policy terms from 10 to 30 years. No cash value component; pure death benefit protection during the chosen term.

**Key features**
- Coverage up to approximately $3 million
- Policy terms of 10, 15, 20, 25, or 30 years
- Broad underwriting tolerance for older applicants (frequently up to age 75)
- In-person medical underwriting through a country-wide panel of examiners
- Multiple rider options including waiver of premium, accidental death, and guaranteed insurability
- Option to convert to CenturyGuard Life Whole without new medical underwriting during the conversion window

**Pricing tier:** Premium
