# QuickSafe Go (QuickSafe)
### Type: Travel Insurance (Limited)

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

QuickSafe Go is the carrier's travel insurance offering, structured as short-term trip coverage. Coverage includes emergency medical, trip cancellation, and baggage loss, with medical coverage capped at approximately $250,000 on the standard tier. The product is not positioned as a full-featured travel insurance line — it is sold as an add-on-style product for customers already on QuickSafe Drive or Nest.

**Key features**
- One-tap purchase from the main app dashboard
- Instant digital policy certificate
- Integration with the customer's existing QuickSafe account
- Single-trip only with a maximum trip length of 60 days

**Pricing tier:** Mid-market
