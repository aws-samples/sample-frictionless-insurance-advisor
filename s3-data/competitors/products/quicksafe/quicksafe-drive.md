# QuickSafe Drive (QuickSafe)
### Type: Auto Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

QuickSafe Drive is the carrier's auto insurance product, offered with third-party, third-party fire and theft, and comprehensive tiers. Liability limits extend up to approximately $300,000 bodily injury and $250,000 property damage on the top tier. The product integrates with a telematics feature in the app that can offer premium adjustments based on driving behaviour.

**Key features**
- In-app quote-to-bind in under three minutes
- Optional telematics-based premium feedback loop
- Photo-based claims submission with same-day acknowledgement
- Modern policy documents designed for in-app reading rather than print

**Pricing tier:** Mid-market
