# QuickSafe Nest Renters (QuickSafe)
### Type: Renters Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

QuickSafe Nest Renters is the tenant variant of the QuickSafe Nest product family. Coverage focuses on personal property and personal liability for renters, with no dwelling component. Personal property coverage is available as a stand-alone figure, and landlord proof-of-insurance is delivered digitally on request.

**Key features**
- Personal property coverage up to approximately $150,000
- Liability coverage up to approximately $500,000
- Digital proof-of-insurance delivered to landlords in-app
- In-app quote-to-bind in under three minutes
- Replacement cost coverage on contents up to policy limits
- Shares claims and policy servicing flows with QuickSafe Nest Home

**Pricing tier:** Mid-market
