# QuickSafe Nest Home (QuickSafe)
### Type: Home Insurance

> **NOTICE:** This document is illustrative and describes a fictional competitor product used for demo and training purposes. It does not represent any real company. Advisors must not quote this content as factual information about real insurers in any customer-facing conversation.

QuickSafe Nest Home is the owner-occupied variant of the QuickSafe Nest product family. Dwelling coverage extends up to approximately $1,000,000, with personal property coverage available as a percentage of dwelling value. Sold through the main QuickSafe app, the product shares policy servicing and claims flows with the renters variant.

**Key features**
- Dwelling coverage up to approximately $1,000,000
- Replacement cost coverage on dwelling and contents up to policy limits
- Personal property coverage selectable at 50%, 70%, or 100% of dwelling value
- Liability coverage up to approximately $500,000
- Optional smart home device integration for premium adjustments
- In-app quote-to-bind, photo-based claims, modern mobile-first policy documents

**Pricing tier:** Mid-market
