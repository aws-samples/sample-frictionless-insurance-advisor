# JetSetUnicorn Travel
### Type: Travel Insurance

**JetSetUnicorn Travel Insurance** offers protection against various risks encountered while traveling abroad, including medical emergencies, trip cancellations, lost luggage, and travel delays. This product provides emergency medical coverage, evacuation services, and reimbursement for unexpected expenses during international trips. Ideal for frequent international travelers, families planning overseas vacations, business professionals on international assignments, senior travelers, and adventure tourists engaging in higher-risk activities abroad.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Geographic Coverage** - Choose from Regional (Europe, Asia, North America), Continental (Europe & Middle East, Americas, Asia & Oceania), or Worldwide coverage options.

2. **Trip Duration** - Select from Single Trip (1-90 days) or Annual Multi-Trip (unlimited trips up to 30, 45, or 60 days each).

3. **Coverage Level** - Options for Basic, Standard, Premium, or Elite coverage tiers with increasing benefit limits.

4. **Medical Coverage** - Select from $50,000, $100,000, $500,000, $1,000,000, or unlimited emergency medical coverage.

5. **Trip Cancellation Coverage** - Choose coverage from $1,500 up to $25,000 for prepaid, non-refundable trip costs.

6. **Traveler Age** - Special coverage options for different age groups: under 30, 31-60, 61-70, 71-80, or 81+ years.

### Optional Coverage

1. **Adventure Sports Coverage** - Extends protection to include high-risk activities such as skiing, scuba diving, mountain climbing, and other adventure sports typically excluded from standard travel policies.

2. **Rental Car Protection** - Provides comprehensive coverage for rental vehicles abroad, eliminating the need for expensive rental agency insurance options.
