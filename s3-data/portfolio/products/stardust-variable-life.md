# StardustVariable Life
### Type: Variable Life Insurance

**StardustVariable Life Insurance** provides permanent coverage combined with cash value allocated across professionally managed investment sub-accounts selected by the policyholder. Cash value and, on some death benefit options, the death benefit itself fluctuate with the performance of the chosen sub-accounts. The product offers the upside potential of market-linked growth with the tax-advantaged structure of a life insurance contract. Best suited for financially sophisticated customers with a long time horizon and tolerance for investment risk: high-income professionals, business owners, customers already maxing out qualified retirement accounts, and those looking to combine estate planning with tax-efficient wealth accumulation. Customers must acknowledge that sub-account values can decline, and that sustained poor performance can require additional premium to keep the policy in force.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Death Benefit** - Select from $500,000, $1,000,000, $2,000,000, $5,000,000, up to $10,000,000 permanent coverage.

2. **Death Benefit Option** - Choose Option A (level face amount), Option B (face amount plus sub-account value), or Option C (face amount plus return of premiums paid).

3. **Sub-Account Allocation** - Allocate cash value across a menu of sub-accounts spanning equity, fixed income, balanced, international, and money market categories. Rebalance or switch allocations subject to policy transaction rules.

4. **Premium Flexibility** - Pay within a flexible premium range, with overfunding allowed up to IRS modified endowment contract limits.

5. **Premium Payment Frequency** - Options for monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or annual premium payments.

6. **Loans and Withdrawals** - Access cash value through policy loans or partial withdrawals, with tax treatment depending on policy structure and cumulative premiums paid.

### Optional Coverage

1. **Guaranteed Minimum Death Benefit Rider** - Ensures a minimum death benefit regardless of sub-account performance, subject to payment of a specified minimum premium.

2. **Dollar Cost Averaging Program** - Automatically transfers a specified amount from a money market sub-account to equity sub-accounts on a regular schedule, reducing the risk of investing a lump sum at a market peak.

3. **Long-Term Care Acceleration Rider** - Allows acceleration of a portion of the death benefit to cover qualifying long-term care needs during the policyholder's lifetime.

4. **Waiver of Premium** - Waives premiums if the policyholder becomes totally disabled, keeping the policy in force and sub-accounts invested through the disability period.

**Important disclosure:** Variable life insurance involves investment risk, including the possible loss of principal. Sub-account values fluctuate with market performance, and poor performance may require additional premium payments to maintain coverage. This product is sold by prospectus only; customers should review the prospectus and discuss suitability with a licensed advisor before purchase.
