# FastUnicorn Motor
### Type: Motor Insurance

**FastUnicorn Motor Insurance** provides financial protection against physical damage and bodily injury resulting from traffic accidents, as well as liability that could arise from vehicle incidents. Coverage options range from basic third-party liability to comprehensive plans that include collision damage, theft protection, and personal accident coverage. This type of product is mandatory for all vehicle owners and particularly valuable for daily commuters, business professionals who drive frequently, families with multiple vehicles, and owners of high-value automobiles.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Coverage Type** - Choose from Third Party Only, Third Party Fire & Theft, or Comprehensive coverage.

2. **Deductible Amount** - Select from $250, $500, $1,000, or $2,000 deductibles for collision and comprehensive claims.

3. **Liability Limits** - Options for $25,000/$50,000, $50,000/$100,000, $100,000/$300,000, or $250,000/$500,000 bodily injury/property damage coverage.

4. **Vehicle Usage** - Select from personal use, commuting, business use, or commercial use classifications.

5. **Annual Mileage** - Choose estimated annual mileage bands: under 5,000, 5,000-10,000, 10,000-15,000, or 15,000+ miles.

6. **Driver Coverage** - Options for named drivers only or any driver over a specified age.

### Optional Coverage

1. **Roadside Assistance** - Offers 24/7 emergency services including towing, battery jump-start, flat tire change, lockout assistance, and fuel delivery.

2. **New Vehicle Replacement** - Provides full replacement cost for new vehicles (less than 2 years old) that are totaled in an accident, rather than depreciated value.
