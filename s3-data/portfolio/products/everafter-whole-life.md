# EverAfter Whole Life
### Type: Whole Life Insurance

**EverAfter Whole Life Insurance** provides permanent lifetime coverage combined with a guaranteed cash value component that grows at a contractually fixed minimum rate. Premiums are level for life, so the cost never increases with age, and the policy builds cash value the policyholder can borrow against or withdraw. The death benefit is guaranteed as long as premiums are paid, regardless of changes in health. EverAfter is built for customers who want lifelong protection with predictable costs and a forced-savings element: estate planning, leaving a legacy for dependents, funding a buy-sell agreement, or creating tax-advantaged cash accumulation that complements retirement savings.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Death Benefit** - Select permanent coverage from $100,000, $250,000, $500,000, $1,000,000, up to $2,500,000.

2. **Premium Payment Schedule** - Choose from standard lifetime pay (premiums paid until death or age 100), 10-pay (paid-up after 10 years), 20-pay (paid-up after 20 years), or paid-up at age 65.

3. **Premium Payment Frequency** - Options for monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or annual premium payments.

4. **Dividend Option** - Choose how policy dividends are applied: paid in cash, used to reduce premiums, left on deposit to accumulate interest, or used to purchase paid-up additional insurance.

5. **Cash Value Access** - Policy loans against accumulated cash value at a guaranteed loan rate, or partial withdrawals subject to policy limits.

6. **Guaranteed Insurability** - Ability to purchase additional permanent coverage at specified life events (marriage, birth of child, home purchase) without new medical underwriting.

### Optional Coverage

1. **Paid-Up Additions Rider** - Allows the policyholder to make additional premium contributions that purchase small blocks of fully paid-up permanent insurance, accelerating both death benefit growth and cash value accumulation.

2. **Accelerated Death Benefit** - Provides access to a portion of the death benefit during the policyholder's lifetime if diagnosed with a terminal illness, chronic illness, or qualifying long-term care need.

3. **Waiver of Premium** - Waives premium payments if the policyholder becomes totally disabled, keeping the policy and its cash value growth intact through the disability period.
