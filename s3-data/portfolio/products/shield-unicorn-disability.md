# ShieldUnicorn Disability
### Type: Disability Insurance

**ShieldUnicorn Disability Insurance** provides income protection for policyholders who become unable to work due to illness or injury. Coverage pays a monthly benefit that replaces a percentage of the policyholder's income during the disability period, helping cover living expenses, mortgage payments, and family financial obligations when earning capacity is interrupted. This product is essential for self-employed professionals, primary household earners, employees without employer-provided disability coverage, and individuals in physically demanding or specialized occupations where loss of ability to work would create significant financial hardship.

### Three-Layer Income Protection (Unicorn's Unique Advantage)

Customers can combine all three products for comprehensive protection:

1. **Layer 1 - Medical expenses**: HealingHorn Health pays for treatment directly (cashless or reimbursement).
2. **Layer 2 - Lump-sum buffer**: Unicorn Health Critical pays $25K-$500K on diagnosis for immediate financial needs.
3. **Layer 3 - Monthly income**: ShieldUnicorn Disability replaces $2K-$15K/month until recovery or retirement.

ShieldUnicorn Disability at $8,000/month provides:

- For a 35-year-old who becomes permanently disabled: potential total benefit of **$2.88M** over 30 years.

**Combined approach**: Lump-sum covers immediate costs + lifestyle adjustment; disability income covers ongoing living expenses indefinitely.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Benefit Period** - Choose the maximum length of time benefits are paid during a single disability claim: 2 years, 5 years, 10 years, or until retirement age (typically 65).

2. **Waiting Period** - Select the elimination period before benefits begin: 30, 60, 90, 180, or 365 days. Longer waiting periods reduce premiums.

3. **Monthly Benefit Amount** - Options ranging from $2,000 to $15,000 per month, typically capped at 60-70% of the policyholder's pre-disability income.

4. **Occupation Class** - Classification from Class 1 (office/professional) through Class 5 (heavy manual labor), affecting both pricing and benefit eligibility.

5. **Definition of Disability** - Choose between Own Occupation (cannot perform your specific job), Any Occupation (cannot perform any job suited to your training), or a hybrid Own Occupation for a set period then Any Occupation.

6. **Coverage Type** - Select Short-Term Disability (up to 2 years), Long-Term Disability (beyond 2 years), or a combined policy.

### Optional Coverage

1. **Cost of Living Adjustment (COLA)** - Automatically increases the monthly benefit during an ongoing claim to keep pace with inflation, protecting purchasing power over long-term disabilities.

2. **Future Purchase Option** - Allows the policyholder to increase coverage amounts at specified intervals or life events (salary increase, promotion, marriage) without new medical underwriting, up to a predefined maximum.

3. **Partial Disability Benefit** - Pays a reduced monthly benefit (typically 50%) when the policyholder can work part-time but not full-time, supporting gradual return to work without financial penalty.
