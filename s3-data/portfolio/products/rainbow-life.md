# Rainbow Life
### Type: Term Life Insurance

**Rainbow Life Insurance** provides financial protection for a specified period (term). It pays a death benefit to beneficiaries if the insured dies during the policy term. This product offers high coverage amounts at affordable premiums with no cash value component. Ideal for young families, parents with dependent children, and individuals with specific financial obligations like mortgages or education expenses who need maximum protection during their income-earning years.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Death Benefit** - Select from $250,000, $500,000, $1,000,000, $1,500,000, or up to $2,000,000 coverage amounts.

2. **Term Length** - Choose from 10, 15, 20, 25, or 30-year term periods.

3. **Premium Payment Frequency** - Options for monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or annual premium payments.

4. **Premium Structure** - Select from level premiums (guaranteed not to increase during the term) or return of premium option (higher initial cost but returns all premiums at end of term if no claim is made).

5. **Conversion Option** - Ability to convert to permanent life insurance before age 70 without evidence of insurability.

### Optional Coverage

1. **Accelerated Death Benefit** - Allows policyholders to access a portion of the death benefit if diagnosed with a terminal illness, providing funds for medical expenses and end-of-life care.

2. **Waiver of Premium** - Waives premium payments if the policyholder becomes totally disabled, ensuring the policy remains in force without additional financial burden during disability.
