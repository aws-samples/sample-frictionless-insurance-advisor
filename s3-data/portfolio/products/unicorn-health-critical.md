# Unicorn Health Critical
### Type: Critical Illness Insurance

**Unicorn Health Critical Insurance** delivers a lump-sum payment upon diagnosis of covered serious illnesses such as cancer, heart attack, or stroke. This payment helps cover medical expenses, treatment costs, and income replacement during recovery. The product bridges gaps in traditional health insurance by providing financial support beyond medical bills. Best suited for working adults aged 30-55, individuals with family history of serious illnesses, and those seeking additional financial security during health crises.

### Why Our Premium May Differ from Competitors
- **Mid-market positioning**: More expensive than StarBasic Shield (budget) but covers 20+ conditions vs StarInsure's 3.
- **Level premiums available**: Unlike StarInsure's stepped premiums that increase with age, we offer fixed premiums for the entire term.
- **Multi-stage benefit**: Unlike StarBasic Shield (single payout, policy terminates), our Comprehensive tier pays at early, intermediate, and major stages — up to 3 separate payouts totaling 300% of sum assured.
- **No premium increase after a claim**: First-stage payout does not affect premiums.
- **Bundle with HealingHorn Health**: 10% premium discount when held together.

## Coverage Amount and Payout Structure

### Lump-Sum Options
- $25,000, $50,000, $100,000, $250,000, or $500,000 per stage.

### Multi-Stage Payout (Comprehensive Tier)
| Stage | Payout | Example Conditions |
|-------|--------|-------------------|
| Early stage | 25% of sum assured | Early-stage cancer (Stage I), minor stroke (TIA), early kidney disease |
| Intermediate stage | 50% of sum assured | Intermediate cancer (Stage II), coronary artery bypass |
| Major stage | 100% of sum assured | Advanced cancer (Stage III/IV), major heart attack, total kidney failure |

- **Total possible payout: Up to 300% of original sum assured** across all stages.
- **Policy continues after early/intermediate stage claim** — not terminated.
- **Reinstatement benefit**: After early-stage claim, coverage for that condition reinstates after 12 months.

### Covered Conditions (Comprehensive Tier — 20+ conditions)
Cancer, heart attack, stroke, kidney failure, major organ transplant, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, Alzheimer's disease, motor neurone disease, aorta surgery, coronary artery bypass, heart valve surgery, major burns, total blindness, total deafness, loss of limbs, coma, paralysis, aplastic anaemia, benign brain tumour, encephalitis.

## Income Replacement vs Lump-Sum

- **Unicorn Health Critical provides lump-sum payouts** — policyholder decides how to use the money (medical bills, income replacement, debt repayment, lifestyle changes).
- **No monthly income replacement** under this product (available via ShieldUnicorn Disability).
- **Hospital cash benefit**: Can be combined with HealingHorn Health's hospital cash add-on for daily income during hospitalisation.

### Why Lump-Sum Works for Income Replacement
- A $250,000 lump sum at major-stage diagnosis can replace ~3-4 years of median income, giving recovery time without financial pressure.
- No requirement to prove income loss or submit monthly documentation (unlike disability products).
- Paid regardless of whether customer returns to work — full flexibility.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Coverage Amount** - Select from $25,000, $50,000, $100,000, $250,000, or $500,000 lump-sum payment options.

2. **Coverage Term** - Choose from 10, 15, 20, or 30-year terms, or until age 65 or 75.

3. **Illness Categories** - Select from Basic (cancer, heart attack, stroke), Standard (adds kidney failure, major organ transplant), or Comprehensive (adds 20+ additional conditions).

4. **Premium Structure** - Choose between level premiums (fixed for the term) or stepped premiums (lower initially but increase with age).

5. **Return of Premium Option** - Option to receive all premiums back if no claim is made by the end of the policy term (higher premium required).

### Optional Coverage

1. **Recovery Care Benefit** - Provides additional monthly payments ($2,000-$5,000/month for up to 12 months) during recovery period after initial diagnosis, supporting ongoing rehabilitation and therapy expenses.

2. **Child Critical Illness Coverage** - Extends protection to policyholder's children (up to age 21) for specified childhood critical illnesses at no additional premium, offering financial support for pediatric medical treatments and parental leave.
