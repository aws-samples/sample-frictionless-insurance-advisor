# GlitterBarn Home Insurance
### Type: Home Insurance

**GlitterBarn Home Insurance** protects homeowners against damage to their property and possessions from perils like fire, theft, and natural disasters. Coverage typically includes dwelling protection, personal property coverage, liability protection, and additional living expenses if temporary relocation is necessary. This product is essential for homeowners, new property buyers, landlords, and anyone with significant investment in their residence and belongings who wants comprehensive protection against financial loss.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Property Value** - Coverage based on the estimated rebuilding cost of the home, ranging from $100,000 to $2,000,000+.

2. **Deductible Amount** - Choose from $500, $1,000, $2,500, or $5,000 deductibles (higher deductibles result in lower premiums).

3. **Personal Property Coverage** - Select coverage for contents at 50%, 70%, or 100% of the dwelling coverage amount.

4. **Liability Protection** - Choose from $100,000, $300,000, $500,000, or $1,000,000 in personal liability coverage.

5. **Property Type** - Options for single-family homes, condominiums, townhouses, rental properties, or vacation homes with specialized coverage for each.

6. **Additional Living Expenses** - Select coverage for 12, 24, or 36 months of temporary housing if your home becomes uninhabitable.

### Optional Coverage

1. **Flood Protection** - Extends coverage to include damage caused by flooding events, which are typically excluded from standard home insurance policies.

2. **Home Business Coverage** - Provides protection for business equipment, inventory, and liability for those operating small businesses from their homes.
