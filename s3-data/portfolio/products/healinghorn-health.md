# HealingHorn Health
### Type: Health Insurance

**HealingHorn Health Insurance** provides comprehensive coverage for medical expenses including hospitalization, outpatient care, specialist consultations, diagnostics, and prescription medications. This product ensures policyholders have access to quality healthcare without facing financial hardship from unexpected medical costs. HealingHorn Health bridges the gap between public healthcare systems and the full spectrum of medical needs, offering flexibility and peace of mind for individuals and families. Best suited for employed individuals and self-employed professionals aged 18-65, families seeking broad medical coverage, and those looking to complement existing public or employer-provided health benefits.

### Why Our Premium May Differ from Competitors
- **Our premium is positioned mid-market** — typically 15-20% lower than BigRival's PrimeCare Health, but 25-35% higher than StarInsure's StarBasic Shield.
- **No premium increase after a claim**: Unlike BigRival which applies claims-based loading of up to 30% after a major claim, our premiums remain stable regardless of claim history.
- **Multi-policy discount**: Customers who also hold Rainbow Life or Unicorn Health Critical receive 10% off HealingHorn Health premiums. Three or more policies earn 15% off.
- **Annual premium review**: Premiums are reviewed annually based on overall portfolio claims experience, not individual claims. Typical annual increase: 3-5% (vs BigRival's 5-8% and StarInsure's 8-12% in recent years).
- **No-claim reward**: Policyholders with no claims in a continuous 2-year period receive a 10% loyalty discount on renewal.

## Coverage Details

### Outpatient Treatments
- **Standard and Premium tiers include outpatient coverage**:
  - GP consultations: Covered up to $80/visit, max 24 visits/year
  - Specialist consultations: Covered up to $200/visit, max 12 visits/year
  - Diagnostic imaging (X-ray, MRI, CT): Covered at 80% up to $5,000/year
  - Prescription medications: Covered up to $3,000/year
  - Physiotherapy: Covered up to $150/session, max 20 sessions/year
- **Essential tier**: Outpatient coverage limited to post-hospitalisation follow-up (90 days after discharge)

### Dental Coverage
- **Premium tier includes basic dental**:
  - Preventive care (cleaning, check-ups): 2 visits/year, covered up to $200/visit
  - Basic restorative (fillings, extractions): Covered at 70%, max $1,500/year
  - Emergency dental (accident-related): Covered at 100% up to $5,000/incident
- **Standard tier**: Emergency dental only (accident-related)
- **Essential tier**: No dental coverage

### Optical/Vision Coverage
- **Premium tier includes vision**:
  - Annual eye exam: Covered up to $150
  - Corrective lenses/frames: $300 allowance every 2 years
  - Contact lenses: $200 allowance/year
- **Standard and Essential tiers**: No optical coverage

### Mental Health Coverage
- **Premium tier**:
  - Inpatient psychiatric: Up to $15,000/year
  - Outpatient psychology/psychiatry: Up to $200/session, max 20 sessions/year ($4,000/year cap)
  - Tele-therapy: Covered at same rates as in-person
  - Employee Assistance Programme integration available for corporate clients
- **Standard tier**:
  - Inpatient psychiatric: Up to $8,000/year
  - Outpatient psychology: Up to $150/session, max 12 sessions/year
- **Essential tier**:
  - Inpatient psychiatric: Up to $4,000/year (emergencies only)

### Maternity Coverage
- **Available as add-on across all tiers** (additional premium: ~$1,200-$2,400/year depending on age):
  - Pre-natal care: All routine check-ups, scans, blood tests covered
  - Delivery (normal): Covered up to $8,000
  - Delivery (caesarean): Covered up to $15,000
  - Complications of pregnancy: Covered at 100% up to policy limit
  - Post-natal care: 6 weeks of follow-up covered
  - Newborn coverage: First 30 days from birth covered under mother's policy
  - **12-month waiting period** for maternity benefits from policy inception
  - IVF pregnancies: Covered for delivery and complications (IVF treatment itself not covered)

### Alternative Treatments
- **Premium tier includes alternative medicine**:
  - Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM): Up to $80/session, max 15 sessions/year
  - Acupuncture: Up to $100/session, max 12 sessions/year
  - Chiropractic: Up to $100/session, max 12 sessions/year
  - Osteopathy: Up to $100/session, max 10 sessions/year
  - Naturopathy: Up to $80/session, max 10 sessions/year
- **Standard tier**: TCM and acupuncture only (same limits)
- **Essential tier**: Not covered

## Claims Process

### Cashless Claims (Direct Billing)
- **Available at all panel hospitals and clinics** — over 800 facilities across our operating markets.
- **Electronic Letter of Guarantee (eLOG)** issued within 2 hours for planned admissions and 30 minutes for emergencies.
- **Pre-authorisation via mobile app**: Submit planned procedures for approval before admission.
- **Panel hospitals**: All major public and private hospitals in our markets. Full list searchable in-app.
- **Non-panel providers**: Reimbursement model applies (submit receipts via app, processed within 5 business days).

### Reimbursement Claims
- Submit via mobile app, web portal, or email.
- Standard processing: 5 business days for straightforward claims.
- Payment directly to policyholder's bank account.
- 95% of health claims settled within 10 business days.

### Claims Settlement Ratio
- Overall health claims settlement ratio: **96.2%** (last published year).
- Top reasons for claim rejection: policy expired (3%), non-disclosure of pre-existing condition (0.5%), excluded treatment (0.3%).

## Income Replacement Options
- **HealingHorn Health does not include income replacement** — this is available via ShieldUnicorn Disability (separate product).
- **Hospital cash benefit add-on**: $100-$500/day during hospitalization (choice of amount), paid in addition to medical expense coverage. Designed to cover incidental expenses, transport, and temporary income loss.
- **Lump-sum critical illness payout**: Available via Unicorn Health Critical (separate product), pays $25,000-$500,000 on diagnosis regardless of actual medical expenses.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Coverage Tier** - Choose from Essential (hospitalization and emergency care), Standard (adds outpatient and specialist visits), or Premium (adds dental, vision, and mental health coverage).

2. **Annual Benefit Limit** - Select from $10,000, $25,000, $50,000, $100,000, or $250,000 in annual maximum benefit coverage.

3. **Deductible Amount** - Choose your annual deductible from $0, $250, $500, $1,000, or $2,500 to balance premium costs against out-of-pocket exposure.

4. **Hospital Network** - Select from Standard Network (in-network hospitals only), Extended Network (broader hospital and clinic access), or Open Access (freedom to choose any licensed healthcare provider).

5. **Reimbursement Model** - Choose between direct billing (insurer pays provider directly) or reimbursement (policyholder pays and claims back expenses).

### Optional Coverage

1. **International Coverage Add-On** - Extends health coverage to medical treatments received abroad, including emergency evacuation and repatriation, ideal for frequent travelers and expatriates.

2. **Chronic Condition Management** - Provides enhanced benefits for ongoing management of chronic conditions such as diabetes, asthma, or cardiovascular disease, including regular check-ups, specialist visits, and prescribed medications.

3. **Maternity Add-On** - Comprehensive pregnancy and delivery coverage including pre-natal, delivery, complications, and post-natal care with 12-month waiting period.

4. **Hospital Cash Benefit** - Daily cash payment during hospitalization to cover incidental expenses and temporary income loss.
