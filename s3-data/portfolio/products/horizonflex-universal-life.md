# HorizonFlex Universal Life
### Type: Universal Life Insurance

**HorizonFlex Universal Life Insurance** provides permanent coverage with flexible premiums and an adjustable death benefit. Policyholders can vary premium payments within contractual limits, and the policy's cash value earns interest at a declared rate with a guaranteed minimum floor. Unlike whole life, HorizonFlex separates the insurance cost from the cash value accumulation, giving policyholders visibility into what they are paying for protection and what is building as equity. Best suited for customers whose income or cash-flow needs may change over time - business owners with uneven income, professionals planning for career transitions, and individuals who want permanent coverage with the ability to adjust contributions to match life circumstances.

### Customer-Selectable Features

1. **Death Benefit** - Select from $250,000, $500,000, $1,000,000, $2,000,000, up to $5,000,000 permanent coverage.

2. **Death Benefit Option** - Choose Option A (level death benefit, where the benefit stays constant as cash value grows) or Option B (increasing death benefit equal to the face amount plus accumulated cash value).

3. **Premium Flexibility** - Pay the target premium, minimum premium to keep the policy in force, or overfund up to IRS-defined limits to accelerate cash value growth.

4. **Premium Payment Frequency** - Options for monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or annual premium payments.

5. **Interest Crediting** - Cash value earns a declared interest rate reset periodically, subject to a contractually guaranteed minimum floor (typically 2% or 3%).

6. **Death Benefit Adjustment** - Ability to increase the death benefit with new underwriting, or decrease it without underwriting, as family and financial circumstances change.

### Optional Coverage

1. **No-Lapse Guarantee Rider** - Keeps the policy in force as long as the minimum no-lapse premium is paid, protecting against policy lapse if interest crediting rates underperform over long periods.

2. **Long-Term Care Acceleration Rider** - Allows the policyholder to accelerate a portion of the death benefit to cover qualifying long-term care expenses during their lifetime.

3. **Waiver of Monthly Deduction** - Waives the monthly insurance cost and policy charges if the policyholder becomes totally disabled, preserving cash value and coverage through the disability period.

4. **Accelerated Death Benefit** - Provides early access to a portion of the death benefit on diagnosis of terminal illness or qualifying chronic condition.
