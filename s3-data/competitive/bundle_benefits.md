## Why Bundling Matters for Health + CI Customers

For customers considering our health and critical illness products together:

1. **Complementary coverage**: HealingHorn Health covers medical expenses (hospital, outpatient, specialists). Unicorn Health Critical provides a separate lump-sum for financial flexibility (income replacement, lifestyle changes, debt clearance).
2. **No double-dipping restriction**: A cancer diagnosis triggers both the CI lump-sum AND activates health coverage for ongoing treatment. They are independent benefits.
3. **Premium efficiency**: The 5% combo bonus plus standard multi-policy discount means the CI coverage is effectively 15-17% cheaper than buying standalone.
4. **Unified claims experience**: Single app, single claims team, coordinated case management.
5. **Portability together**: If moving from another insurer, waiting period credits apply across all ported policies.
