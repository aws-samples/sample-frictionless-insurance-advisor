## Cashless Claims Infrastructure

### Our Network
- **800+ panel hospitals and clinics** across all operating markets.
- **Electronic Letter of Guarantee (eLOG)** — fully digital, no paper.
- **Pre-authorisation via app**: Customers can submit planned procedures for pre-approval before admission.
- **Emergency eLOG**: Issued within 30 minutes for emergency admissions.
- **Planned admission eLOG**: Issued within 2 hours.
- **Excess over eLOG**: If the final bill exceeds the guaranteed amount, the balance is processed as a reimbursement claim (5-day turnaround).

## Premium Stability and No-Claim Benefits

### Premium Stability Guarantee
- **No individual claims-based loading.** Our premiums are community-rated based on overall portfolio performance.
- Annual premium adjustments based on medical inflation and portfolio experience (typically 3-5% annually).
- Contrast with BigRival: Individual claims-based loading of up to 30% after a major health claim.
- Contrast with StarInsure: Annual increases of 8-12% due to smaller risk pool and adverse selection.

## Pre-Existing Condition Handling

### Our Approach
- Standard 12-month waiting period for pre-existing conditions.
- **After 12 months: pre-existing conditions are fully covered** at same benefit levels as any other condition.
- No premium surcharge or loading for pre-existing conditions once past waiting period.
- Full disclosure at application; non-disclosure may void coverage.

### Competitor Comparison: Pre-Existing Conditions

| Feature | Unicorn | BigRival | StarInsure |
|---------|---------|----------|-----------|
