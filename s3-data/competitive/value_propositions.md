# Unicorn Insurance - Value Propositions

Advisor talking points for positioning Unicorn Insurance against alternatives. Each value proposition below summarizes a specific, concrete reason a customer or prospect should consider Unicorn. Use these as framing during discovery conversations and when a prospect asks "why you?"

## 1. Fast, Transparent Claims Handling

Claims are where an insurance relationship is proven, and we invest heavily in making ours predictable. On average, we acknowledge a new claim within 4 business hours, assign an adjuster within 1 business day, and settle 95% of straightforward claims within 10 business days. Customers receive SMS and in-app status updates at every stage, so they are never left wondering where their file stands.

For health insurance specifically: cashless claims via eLOG are processed in under 2 hours for planned admissions and under 30 minutes for emergencies. Our 96.2% claims settlement ratio exceeds the industry average of 94%.

## 2. Broad Coverage Across Seven Product Lines

We underwrite across life, health, critical illness, motor, home, disability, and travel under a single brand and a single customer file. That breadth matters when a customer's needs change - a new home, a new child, a new role - because we can expand protection without asking the customer to start over with a new carrier. It also means one advisor, one login, and one claims hotline for the entire household.

## 3. Meaningful Multi-Policy Savings

Our bundling discounts are structured to reward genuine consolidation rather than offering marketing-grade token savings. Two-policy bundles typically earn up to 12% off, three or more policies earn up to 15% off, and specific combinations such as Health + Critical Illness or Life + Disability carry their own stacking discounts. Full details live in `bundle_benefits.md`, but the headline is that customers who consolidate with us see discounts that meaningfully affect their annual premium.

## 4. Digital-First Experience Backed by Human Advisors

Customers can buy, manage, and claim entirely through our mobile app and web portal - quote, e-sign, upload documents, track claims, update beneficiaries, and download tax statements. What sets us apart from purely digital players like StarInsure is that customers also have access to qualified human advisors for complex decisions, claims support, and policy reviews.

## 5. Premium Stability (No Claims-Based Loading)

Unlike BigRival which penalises customers with up to 30% premium loading after a major claim, our premiums are community-rated and based on overall portfolio performance. A customer who makes a claim today pays the same premium next year as a customer who didn't. This is one of our most powerful talking points when speaking to customers who have had claims experiences with premium-loading carriers.

## 6. Comprehensive Health Coverage Including Outpatient, Dental, and Mental Health

Our HealingHorn Health product (Standard and Premium tiers) includes outpatient coverage, specialist consultations, diagnostic imaging, and prescription medications — not just hospitalisation. The Premium tier adds dental, optical, and full mental health coverage including tele-therapy. This is significantly broader than BigRival's equivalent tier and addresses coverage gaps that customers commonly discover only when they need care.

## 7. Day-One Accident Coverage with Industry-Standard Waiting Periods

All policies provide day-one coverage for accidents and emergencies. General illness has a 30-day waiting period (industry standard). Pre-existing conditions have a 12-month waiting period after which they are fully covered — unlike StarInsure which permanently excludes pre-existing conditions. We also offer portability credit: customers switching from other insurers can get credit for time already served on waiting periods.

## 8. Income Protection Options Across Product Lines

While HealingHorn Health provides a hospital cash benefit (daily cash during admission) and Unicorn Health Critical provides lump-sum payouts on diagnosis, customers who need true monthly income replacement can add ShieldUnicorn Disability. This combination gives customers three layers of financial protection during health crises:
1. Medical expenses covered (HealingHorn Health)
2. Lump-sum financial buffer (Unicorn Health Critical)  
3. Ongoing monthly income replacement (ShieldUnicorn Disability)

No single competitor offers this integrated three-layer approach.
