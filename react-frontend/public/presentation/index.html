The challenge

Why Insurance Advisors Need AI

Complexity & Information Overload

  • Insurance documents are complex, jargon-heavy, and difficult to interpret quickly
  • Advisors manage hundreds of products across multiple providers — impossible to know everything
  • Manual comparison is time-consuming and error-prone

Lost Momentum & Revenue

  • When customers ask questions, advisors often say "let me get back to you" and wait for the insurer — losing momentum and trust
  • Customers expect instant, personalized answers — not callbacks days later
  • Advisors miss cross-sell/upsell opportunities due to lack of holistic coverage visibility

Competitive pressure

Digital-first insurers and aggregators are raising the bar — advisors who can't deliver instant, informed answers lose deals to those who can.

The solution

Frictionless Insurance Assistance
